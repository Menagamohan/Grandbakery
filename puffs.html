Welcome to Grand Bakery - Puffs


Grand Bakery is a wholesale bakery based in Pondicherry, provides high quality of
fresh baked, frozen baked and frozen unbaked products, since 1963. All our products
are hand made, and we use our own home-style recipes. We believe that quality and
freshness are key ingredients in our Products.
