    Welcome to Grand Bakery


    Grand Bakery is a one-of-a-kind Pondicherry institution that opened in 1963 out of a hunger for
    delicious baked products.

    Our reputation for making delicious pastries, such as the now-iconic "black forest pastry," helped us
    to swiftly gain a foothold in the market. These pastries are also available in a variety of other
    flavours, including pineapple, butterscotch, and strawberry. The demand for these has skyrocketed.
    Within no time at all, word of these fresh cream layered pastries' incredible moistness and flavour
    had spread throughout the city. Our fresh baked variety attracted a loyal following that frequented us
    regularly. Croissants, puffs, buns, breads, and even special occasion cakes. Chicken tikka, chicken
    curry, potatoes, vegetables, and paneer cheese are just some of the delicious contents that can be
    found in these croissants and puffs. For those of you who are watching your sugar intake, we also
    have sugar-free cookies available.



    About Us

    Grand Bakery is a wholesale bakery based in Pondicherry, provides high quality of fresh baked,
    frozen baked and frozen unbaked products, since 1963. All our products are hand made, and we use
    our own home-style recipes. We believe that quality and freshness are key ingredients in our
    Products.
